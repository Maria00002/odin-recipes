Desciption

Pizza is a dish of Italian origin consisting of a flattened disk of bread dough topped with come combination of olive oil, oregano, tomato, olives, mozzarella or other cheese, and many other ingredients, baked in an oven headed to a very high temperature and served hot.

PIzza usually has three main elements: crust, sauce, and toppings. All of them have a variety of preparation methods.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Olive Oil
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Parmesan CHeese
  • Italian pepperoni
  • Italian Sausage
  • Sliced black olives

Steps

  1. Flatten the dough ball and stretch out into a round shape
  2. Brush the dough top with olive oil
  3. Spread with tomato sauce and sprinkle with toppings
  4. Slide pizza into the over
  5. Bake pizza in the 475 Fahrenheit degree oven, until the curst is browned and the cheese is golden
